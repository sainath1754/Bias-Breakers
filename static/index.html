How It Works

Three Steps to Fairer AI

📊
1 · Detect
We compute four fairness metrics — Disparate Impact, Statistical Parity Difference, Equal Opportunity, and Average Odds — to quantify exactly how biased your model is.
🔍
2 · Explain
SHAP-based feature attribution pinpoints which columns are driving the bias. Not jargon — a clear breakdown your whole team can understand and act on.
⚖️
3 · Fix
Reweighing adjusts training sample weights so underrepresented groups carry equal influence. Bias removed before deployment — with minimal accuracy loss.
